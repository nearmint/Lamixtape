Lamixtape — monthly curated music mixtapes

Hi and welcome to Lamixtape.

Between 2011 and 2022, we released mixtapes every month, curated by our roster of curators and incredible guests. Our foundational reason for building Lamixtape was that we're really excited about sharing music. If we had a central goal, it was to feed your ears and curiosity with as much quality and diversity as possible.

Lamixtape has no bullshit, no ads, no sponsored posts, and no paywalls. If you enjoy our mixtapes, please consider what we do.

If you really want to see what we’re about, go and explore our 360+ mixtapes.

And remember, getting lost can be a good thing.

PS: we’re not on social media, but you can reach us .
